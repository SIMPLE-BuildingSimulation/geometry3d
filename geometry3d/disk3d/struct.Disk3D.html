Fields

centre: Point3D

The location of the center of the Disk3D

normal: Vector3D

The normal of the Disk3D

radius: f64

The external radius of the Disk3D

inner_radius: f64

The radius of the inner hole of the Disk3D

phi_zero: Vector3D

The axis that marks phi as Zero

phi_max: f64

The angle described by the Disk3D. A value of 2.*PI implies a full Disk3D; a value of less than that, a partial Disk3D.

transform: Option<Rc<Transform>>

A pointer to the Transform associated with this Disk3D
